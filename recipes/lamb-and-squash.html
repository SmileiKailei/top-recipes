Lamb and Summer Squash

Description

Ground lamb and summer squash spiced with freshly ground coriander and cumin served on a bed of homemade hummus.

Ingredients

HUMMUS

  • 2 15.5-oz. cans chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 garlic clove, corasley chopped
  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 3 Tbsp. frech lemon juice
  • 1 1/4 tsp. kosher salt

LAMB AND ASSEMBLY

  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground coirander
  • 1 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for plating
  • 1 lb. ground lamb
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1 lb. summer squash and/or zucchini, cut into 3/4" pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley or cilantro

Steps

HUMMUS

  1. Process chickpeas in a food processor until powdery clumps form, about 30sec.
  2. Add garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and salt and process until smooth
  3. With motor running, drizzle in ½ cup water and process until hummus is very smooth, light, and creamy.
  4. Spoon onto a large plate.

LAMB AND ASSEMBLY

  1. Mix together salt, coriander, cumin, and red pepper flakes (if using) in a small bowl
  2. Heat 1 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet over medium-high
  3. Add lamb and press into a large 1¼"-thick patty; sprinkle with half of spice mixture and half of garlic
  4. Cook, without moving, until browned and crisp, about 5 minutes. Turn over in pieces and cook until other side is browned and crisp, about 3 minutes.
  5. Continue to cook, breaking up and stirring, until cooked through, about 2 minutes longer.
  6. Using a slotted spoon, scoop lamb over hummus.
  7. Pour out and discard fat and wipe out skillet. Heat 1 Tbsp. oil over high
  8. Cook squash in a single layer, undisturbed, until browned, about 4 minutes
  9. Sprinkle with remaining spice mixture and garlic and cook, stirring, until squash is coated and cooked through, about 3 minutes
  10. Spoon squash over lamb. Drizzle with more oil and top with herbs. Serve with pita
