Balsamic Caprese Portobellos

Description

Portobello mushroom caps topped with mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil leaves, baked, then drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • Large portobello mushroom caps
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil
